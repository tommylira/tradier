Equities.com Trading FAQs

What is Equities.com Trading?

Equities.com Trading is a new service from Equities.com that provides access to unlimited commission-free stock trading via Equities.com through our partnership with Tradier Brokerage, Inc, a FINRA- and SIPC-registered broker dealer. Using Equities.com Trading, users can now easily access stock trading via Equities.com articles, company profiles, and other content.

What are the requirements to sign up for Equities.com Trading?

To sign up for trading via Equities.com Trading, an individual must be a US resident 18 years of age or older, have a valid social security number, and credit card. Additional requirements are outlined by Tradier Brokerage, Inc at https://tradier.com.

How do I sign up for Equities.com Trading

To sign up for Equities.com Trading, please visit https://www.equities.com/trading and click on the “Apply Now” button.

Who is Tradier Brokerage, Inc?

Tradier Brokerage, Inc. (TBI) is a FINRA and SIPC registered broker dealer that provides cloud-based brokerage services dozens of 3rd party investment, advisory, and financial media firms, including Equities.com. Equities.com has entered into an agreement with TBI to provide access to unlimited commission free stock trading via Equities.com. You can learn more about Tradier Brokerage here: https://tradier.com

What is the cost of trading via Equities.com Trading?

Equities.com has entered into an agreement with Tradier Brokerage to provide access to unlimited commission-free stock trading for $29.95 month. This offer is available only for subscriptions that are setup via Equities.com. The unlimited commission-free stock trading offer will remain in effect as long as your subscription remains in good standing.

What can I trade via my Equities.com Trading subscription?

Tradier Brokerage currently allows trading of stocks and ETFs traded on the NASDAQ, NYSE, or BATS exchanges. Tradier Brokerage does not currently offer trading of OTC or non-US securities.

Can I trade options via my Equities.com Trading subscription?

No. While Tradier Brokerage offers options trading, we have not enabled this functionality via Equities.com Trading. We are exploring options to add this capability in the future.

Does Equities.com provide any trading APIs?

No. Equities.com does not expose any APIs for trading. If you are interested in building your own trading application, you can learn more by visiting Tradier’s website at: https://tradier.com

Is Equities.com Trading compatible with mobile devices?

Yes. Equities.com Trading is compatible with most major mobile devices, including most Apple and Android smartphones and tablets.

Is Equities.com Trading secure?

All trading functions for Equities.com Trading are hosted and managed by Tradier Brokerage, Inc. Tradier Brokerage’s systems meet all FINRA and SIPC requirements for brokerage system security. For more information on Tradier security, visit Tradier’s webpage at: https://tradier.com

If I have an existing Tradier account, how can I take advantage of your $29.95 unlimited commission free trading offer?

To take advantage of Equities.com Trading’s $29.95 per month unlimited commission free trading offer, you must open a new account via Equities.com. Existing Tradier accounts cannot be transferred into Equities.com’s promotional pricing unfortunately.

How can I cancel my Equities.com Trading subscription?

To cancel your Equities.com Trading account, please contact support@equities.com. We will verify your identity and flag your subscription for cancellation. Upon cancellation, you will no longer have access to unlimited commission-free stock trading for $29.95 and your access to trading from Equities.com will be disabled at the end of your current pay period.

After your Equities.com Trading subscription is cancelled, you will continue to have direct access to your Tradier Brokerage account by visiting https://equities-com.tradier.com. All subsequent trades will be charged on a commission basis, subject to Tradier Brokerage’s terms and conditions. If you have questions about your Tradier account, please contact Tradier support at service@tradierbrokerage.com or (980) 272-3880.

I have questions about my trading account. Who should I contact?

If you have any questions about your Tradier brokerage account, please contact Tradier support at service@tradierbrokerage.com or (980) 272-3880.

My Tradier brokerage account was denied. Will I be charged?

No. Equities.com will only charge you if your Tradier brokerage application was approved. If you have any questions about your Tradier brokerage account, please contact Tradier support at service@tradierbrokerage.com or (980) 272-3880.
